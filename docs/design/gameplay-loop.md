# Gameplay Loop

## Exploration and Preparation

- Explore a procedurally generated world.
- Gather resources and build defenses to prepare for hordeling attacks.
- Place defenders and structures to protect key village areas.

## Horde Timing

- The calendar advances as the player moves.
- The horde attacks on the 30th day of the month.
- Seasonal changes drive the cadence of attacks and recovery windows.

## Defense Phase

- Hordelings arrive in waves and grow stronger over time.
- Defeating the attackers and keeping peasants alive is critical to survival.
